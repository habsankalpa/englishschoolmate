Documents

Project Charter

Project Charter document is the first structured document among a set of documents in the Research project module. The main focus of the Project Charter is to get a basic idea on the outline of the research topic, the importance of research problem and also to review on the general proposed solution structure. Although this is not an assessed submission, it is important to create an organized project charter to successfully proceed with the upcoming work.

Proporsal Document

Project proposal is the evaluation associated with the Research project module. For the above evaluation, students have submit a group report and four individual reports. The project proposal report is evaluated by the supervisor and the co-supervisor.

Research Paper

Once the project is complete, the students are expected to prepare a research paper based on the project carried out. A common mark is allocated for all members of each project group for the research paper and the 10% from the final mark is allocated for the research paper.

Final Document

Final project thesis is the final evaluation of the project. For the above evaluation, the groups are expected to submit a single group report and four individual reports.
