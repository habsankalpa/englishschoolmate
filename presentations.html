Project proposal is the first evaluation associated with the Research Project module. For the above evaluation, students have to submit reports and also do a presentation. The project proposal presentation is evaluated by a three member panel.

Progress presentation - 1

For the progress review 1 students have to do a presentation and a product demonstration. By the time of evaluation, the students are expected to complete at least 50% of the project work. Progress review 1 is evaluated by a three member evlution panel.
